TiDB Performance Challenge

Welcome to TiDB Performance Challenge homepage! Follow the guide on this page to participate in this program. Before getting on board, let’s check out something that may help you in competitions.
TiDB Performance Challenge launched by PingCAP will start on November 4, 2019 and end on February 4, 2020, lasting three months. You can participate inindividually or with a team. When this season ends, you can redeem the prizes with the Contribution Credit (CC) you got!
We believe that each challenge you successfully get through can raise TiDB to a higher level in terms of stability, performance and usability!

HIGHLIGHTS

Step-by-step Challenge

Newbie to TiDB? Don’t worry,「Easy」Issues can help you get on board quickly! After you win enough points, then you can continue to unlock 「Medium」and「Hard」Issues.

Mentors on the Way

Difficulty goes up, support comes out. We have mentors to help you with「Medium」and「Hard」Issues. How to communicate with them? The slack channel is ready for you. Full support along the way can promise you a fruitful challenging experience.

Bountiful Prizes

You can redeem the Contribution Credit for the prizes when the season ends! From the tickets of the TiDB important events to the TiDB swags, you can redeem all as long as you have enough winning points. Besides, we will award the Top 3 winners a customized certificate to recognize their full engagement in this project!
